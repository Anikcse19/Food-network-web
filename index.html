About Food Nutrition

Nutrition is defined as the processes by which an animal or plant takes in and utilises food substances. Essential nutrients include protein, carbohydrate, fat, vitamins, minerals and electrolytes. Normally, 85% of daily energy use is from fat and carbohydrates and 15% from protein. In humans, nutrition is mainly achieved through the process of putting foods into our mouths, chewing and swallowing it.

Procedure of Chicken Chap

Procedure of Kacchi Biriyani

Chop mutton about 150 gm size each with a good quality of the sharp knife. What About Burger?

The hamburger first appeared in the 19th or early 20th century.
The modern hamburger was a product of the culinary needs of a
society rapidly changing due to industrialization and the emergence
of the working class and middle class with the resulting demand
for mass-produced, affordable food that could be consumed
outside of the home.

What About Shwarma?

History of Shawarma Shawarma is a Levantine Arab meat
preparation, where lamb, chicken, turkey, beef, veal, or
mixed meats are placed on a spit (commonly a vertical
spit in restaurants), and may be grilled for as long as a
day. Shavings are cut off the block of meat for serving,
and the remainder of the block of meat is kept heated
on the rotating spit.
